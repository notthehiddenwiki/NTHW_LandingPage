About Project

You are at Not The Hidden Wiki. The project was created because we believe that knowledge should be free!
So we collected many valuable links from various specialists in their fields and created this wiki. Regardless of whether you are just starting your adventure with cybersecurity or you have been in this world for a long time, you will definitely find something for yourself on our GitHub.

This website cannot exist without you! So if you want to contribute to expanding the database of links with useful knowledge (these may be tools, publications, recordings, etc.), send the list to: notthehiddenwiki@firemail.cc or write to me on Discord (@miblak).
